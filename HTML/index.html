About DSC


Helping students bridge the gap between theory and practice.

Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or
graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge
in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

Our Focus


Developer Student Club CUIET is inspired by Google Developers Family.
We engage with student developers through our hack events, workshops
and meetups. Our goal is to create a local ecosystem
of programmers and technologically-aware learners in and around the Campus.

What we do?


We organise tech talks on latest technologies being used in
industries.We conduct hackathons and conferences for honing student's programming
skills . We work on various technical domains and try to
make use of Google Technologies to empower the audience around us.
